In a parallax website, background images or sections may move at a slower rate than foreground content, creating a sense of perspective and immersion. This technique is often used to tell a story, showcase products, or provide a unique and interactive browsing experience. Parallax scrolling can add a modern and dynamic feel to a website, enhancing user engagement and making the design more memorable.
